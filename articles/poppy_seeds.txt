Yes, poppy seeds contain narcotics.

Poppy seeds are used to add flavor to many baked goods, salads, and pasta dishes; ranging from lemon bread to chicken salad. 

Poppy seeds are produced by the opium poppy plant, a plant that has been famous since ancient times for containing psychoactive chemicals. 

All parts of the opium poppy, including the seeds, contain the drugs morphine and codeine. 

In fact, eating poppy seeds can lead you to test positive on a drug test. 

Many government agencies know this fact and therefore discourage people from eating poppy seeds who are required to take drug tests.
